Climate Change

The difference between Climate Change and Weather

It's simple. Weather is the climate of a specific region over a short period of time, And climate is the weather of a specific region over a longer period of time (30 years or more).

What is climate change?

A simple definition of climate change is a change in Earth's overall climate.
